Binaural Beats
What Are They?close
  • Our brains create electricity forming patterns known as brain waves.

  • Each brain wave is associated with a certain state of mind.

  • Binaural Beats unconsciously make our brainwaves match whatever frequency of binaural beat is being played, thus creating a state of mind we would like to experience.
How They Work
It's Science!close
  • One frequency in one ear...
  • A different frequency in the other ear.
  • Your brain calculates the difference and starts resonating that frequency, putting your brain in a certain state of mind - this is the magic behind binaual beats!
